Our Story

The craft, the farmers, and the neighborhood we serve

Brewed Awakening began in a converted brick warehouse with a simple idea: exceptional coffee should be transparent, equitable, and inspiring. Founders Lila and Marco spent two years traveling through Latin America and East Africa forging direct trade relationships with growers and learning the nuances of terroir.

Today, our roastery anchors a community of artists, entrepreneurs, and everyday regulars. Every bean is roasted on a Loring S15 Falcon to accentuate natural sweetness while reducing our carbon footprint by 80%. We reinvest 5% of profits into origin-country education funds and local micro-grants.

Intentional sourcing & sustainability
