Our Story

Bean Haven was born from a friendship that started over a shared love of rich espresso. We source beans directly from farmers, roast in small batches, and celebrate community with every cup.

From pop-up carts to our flagship cafe, our mission has always been to make specialty coffee approachable and inviting.

Values We Brew By

  • • Sustainable sourcing and fair partnerships
  • • Inclusive spaces that welcome everyone
  • • Continuous craft and quality
